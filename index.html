Social Hub


This is a website where users can come and feel safe to express their issues with the community. There are also guidance and counseling members within the group readily available to discuss issues you consider too private.

Services offered

  • Community Support
  • Guidance and Counseling
  • Peer Discussions
  • Resource Sharing
  • Workshops and Webinars

Description

  1. Provide a safe space for users to express their issues openly.
  2. Build a community where members can share and support each other.
  3. Include guidance and counseling members readily available for private discussions.
  4. Encourage respectful and supportive interactions among users.
  5. Offer confidential communication channels for sensitive topics.
  6. Promote mental health awareness and resources within the community.
  7. Facilitate group discussions as well as one-on-one counseling sessions.
  8. Ensure privacy and security to build trust among users.
